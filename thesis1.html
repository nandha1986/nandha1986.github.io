The Problem


I was working with a srishti school of design on a community project. The aim of the tool was to help build a visualization tool to help the general public get access to information about basic amenities including water, pollution overall health of their neighbourhood.


Problem

[the product has been renamed due to NDA purpose]

Currently users did not have any ways to access this data and even they did, making sense of the information was very difficult because there was no way comprehend such large data set unless they are visualized in a meaningful and useful way. I was involved in building a tool that integrates the sensors data to a visualisation tool. The visualization tool had the ability to break information and present in a consumable fashion.


My Role


I was involved in the entire design effort for this project. Right from research to user testing was carried out at a breakneck speed. This project was my final year thesis project - duration 2 montsh

Conclusion

The aim of this project was to make the general public understand the health of any wards (an administrative division of a city or borough that typically elects and is represented by a councillor or councillors). With this project we have just scratched the surface in terms of data that we can provide. My aim is to build more simpler visulisation that can be used to infer data.
